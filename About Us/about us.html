About Us

  • It all started back in 2019 when two LPU students, Rohan Pandey and Shashank decided they wanted to make life easier by changing the way India eats - all with just a tap! With their idea of ’hyperlocal food delivery’, all they needed was the tech to power it and were introduced to Rahul Jaimini, who brought this vision to life with the first website. And with this, Bhukkad was launched as a food ordering & delivery platform.
  • In August of 2019, Bhukkad began operations by signing up a few restaurants in Jalandhar, Punjab. Soon enough, the first team of Hunger Saviors came into action to deliver food within 40 minutes. Shortly after, Bhukkad raised its first round of funding and launched the app in May of 2020. Fun fact: The technology that delivers great food right to your doorstep was completely developed in-house!
  • With love and support from consumers, Bhukkad expanded far and wide, first through the entire city of Jalandhar and then across the entire country. As they say, the rest is history.
  • To ensure and focus on better consumer experience, Bhukkad has launched a platform for partners, wherein Bhukkad partners can order premium packaging material at a reasonable cost and help us in providing an awesome experience for the consumers.
